Anil Thanki

 

Anil is a Senior Bioinformatician at the European Bioinformatics Institute before he worked as a Postdoctoral Research Scientist (2020 - 2022) and Scientific Programmer (2011 - 2020) at the Earlham Institute.

He has PhD in Bioinformatics from the University of East Anglia, UK, Master of Science in Bioinformatics from the University of Leicester, UK and Bachelor of Science in Bioinformatics from Saurashtra University, India.

His area of interest is Bioinformatics tools development, with an interest in Open Science. He is also interested in web-based Data Visualisation, especially using D3.js.
